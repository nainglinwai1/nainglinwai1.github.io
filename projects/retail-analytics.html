Retail Analytics

AI-powered customer behavior analysis, footfall tracking, and heatmap generation for retail stores — turning cameras into business intelligence.

ClientRetail Chain Operator
Duration3 Months
RoleAI Engineer

The Problem

A retail chain with 50+ stores lacked visibility into in-store customer behavior. They relied on manual observation and POS data alone, missing critical insights about dwell time, traffic patterns, and conversion rates. They needed a privacy-preserving, scalable analytics solution.

The Solution

I built a privacy-first computer vision analytics pipeline that processes in-store camera feeds to generate actionable insights. The system tracks anonymized customer movement paths, generates heatmaps of high-traffic zones, measures dwell time at product displays, and correlates footfall with POS transaction data — all without storing any personally identifiable information.

Key Challenges

  • Privacy Compliance: Designed a system that processes all video on-device, transmitting only anonymized metadata — no images or video ever leave the store.
  • Multi-Camera Tracking: Implemented cross-camera person re-identification to track customer journeys across different store zones.
  • Real-Time Dashboards: Built Grafana dashboards with sub-second update latency for store managers to monitor live traffic.
